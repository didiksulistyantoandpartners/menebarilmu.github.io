About Me

Greetings! I am Prof. Dr. sc. agr. Ir. Didik Sulistyanto, an esteemed professor with a profound specialization in Biotechnology and Biological Control, particularly emphasizing Entomopathogenic Nematodes and Integrated Pest Management. Having attained my Dr. sc.agr. degree cum laude from the distinguished Christian Albrecht University of Kiel, Germany, my academic and professional trajectory has been extensively dedicated to groundbreaking research in biological pest control. Throughout my distinguished career, I have successfully spearheaded numerous prominent research initiatives sponsored by esteemed international and national organizations, including the International Foundation for Science (IFS, Sweden), World University Services (WUS, Germany), DGHE Indonesia, Toray Science Foundation (Japan), and RISPRO-LPDP-KEMENKEU Indonesia. My scholarly endeavors focus on pioneering innovative biotechnological approaches to manage agricultural pests and promote organic farming practices sustainably.

As a committed educator, researcher, and consultant, my contributions extend significantly to global and regional partnerships. My previous leadership roles include serving as the Education and Culture Attach√© at the Indonesian Embassy in Thailand, coordinating ASEAN educational forums, and providing expert advisory services to numerous universities and institutions, both domestically and internationally.
